## üìã What is Clockify?

Clockify is a time tracking application designed for teams and freelancers to track work hours across projects and tasks. Founded in 2017, it gained popularity by offering comprehensive time tracking features completely free for unlimited users, differentiating itself from competitors with usage-based pricing.

### Key Features Overview
- Time tracking with timers and manual entry
- Project and task organization
- Team time management
- Basic reporting and timesheets
- Browser extensions and mobile apps
- Integrations with project management tools
- Unlimited users on free plan
- GPS tracking for mobile work
